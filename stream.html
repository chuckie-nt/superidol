STREAMING

What Is Streaming?

Streaming Media

Streaming media is multimedia that is delivered through the internet or any other technological medium continuously manner from a source, with little or no intermediate storage in network elements. It also refers to the delivery method of content, rather than the content itself.

Streaming Platforms

An example of a streaming platform is an on-demand online entertainment source, a great example of a streaming platform is Hulu, Netflix, Youtube, and Twitch. And while these examples may not be in the same league altogether by definition all of them can be considered as a streaming platforms.

REASON

Why Do People Watch Streams?

For Entertainment

The three main reasons why people watch streams are because for entertainment, to be part of a community, and to improve in the specific work they are watching through watching streams.

Live Videos > Reading a Blog

Live streaming has more audience engagement than a blog, it’s a great way to interact with the streamer and the audience unlike blogs or pre-recorded videos which are only one-way communication.

Biggest Video Platform

Not only youtube is the biggest video platform on the planet the videos themselves are sharable, videos can be spread far and wide very easily.

9.3 Billions Hours Watched

Many people use Facebook regularly, so sharing and watching video content is a natural extension of the service.
